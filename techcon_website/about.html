About TechCon 2024

History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small technology meetup, bringing together innovators and tech enthusiasts to share ideas and showcase prototypes.
The pivotal moment for TechCon came in 2018, when it went global, attracting thousands of attendees and speakers from around the world. Topics broadened to include emerging fields such as blockchain, virtual reality (VR), and 5G technology. The conference also introduced dedicated startup zones, where young companies could pitch ideas to investors and gain visibility. By 2014, it grew into an annual conference featuring keynotes, workshops, and exhibitions on emerging technologies like AI and cybersecurity.
In 2018, TechCon went global, attracting speakers, startups, and attendees worldwide, while expanding its focus to include fields like blockchain and VR. Today, TechCon is a premier event for tech innovation, networking, and industry insights, shaping the future of technology on a global scale.
